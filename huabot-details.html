Huabot 🤖 - A Facebook Messenger Chatbot



Project information

  • Category: Chatbot
  • Project date: 08, October, 2020

Huabot is a chatbot operating as Harokopio University's pregraduate student advisor. It helps students with matters regarding the program studies of Informatics and Telematics Department and other operations of Harokopio University. Developed with Python and Rasa Framework.
